The Programme

Two sessions. One journey.

Session One · May 2025
The Leader Within
Reconnecting with Your Purpose

Before you can lead others well, you need to know who you are beyond your job title. This session takes you back to your core — your values, your identity, and the purpose that makes your leadership meaningful.

  • Reconnect with your core values and what truly drives you
  • Distinguish between who you are and what you do
  • Leave with a clear, personal leadership intention
Session Two · June 2025
Perform Well, Feel Well
Sustainable Success for Women Leaders

Purpose without sustainability burns out. This session gives you the tools to perform at your best without running on empty — building a leadership life that is both ambitious and nourishing.

  • Identify your personal energy patterns and depletion triggers
  • Build boundaries that protect performance, not limit it
  • Leave with a sustainable performance plan you'll actually use

Your Facilitator
Alicia Yoon
Organisational Psychologist · Leadership Coach · Barre Instructor

Hi, I'm Alicia — an organisational psychologist, leadership coach, barre instructor, and recovering overachiever.

A few years ago, I hit a wall. Performing well on the outside, quietly falling apart on the inside as I felt exhausted, disconnected, just going through the motions. Finding my way back took therapy, coaching, and a lot of honest conversations with myself about what I actually wanted.

Since then I've worked with women leaders and entrepreneurs across APAC, facilitated corporate workshops, and spoken at panels and keynotes on purpose, performance, and wellbeing. Because these themes are personal.

That's why I created this series. Not just as a coach, but as someone who's been there. If you've been showing up for everyone else, this is your moment to show up for yourself.
